
TIMER0 is used for the timing of the Arduino [millis(), micros() - see C:\Program Files (x86)\Arduino\hardware\arduino\avr\cores\arduino\wiring.c].

TIMER0 - 8-bit
TIMER1 - 16-bit
TIMER2 - 8-bit [can be externally triggered]

Arduino Atmega328p 	Timer

3		d3			TIMER2B,

5		d5			TIMER0B,
6		d6			TIMER0A,

9		b1			TIMER1A,
10		b2			TIMER1B,
11		b3			TIMER2A,
